About & Disclaimer

Hi! I'm Jenna, a Computer Science student. I built this platform because when I was studying for my SC and HSC exams, I struggled to find clear, well-structured CS notes online. Most resources were either too technical, too scattered, or simply not tailored to our local curriculum.

The Study Lounge is my way of giving back. Everything here is written by me — a student, not a teacher or tutor. I'm simply sharing my understanding of the topics I've studied.

Please note: The notes on this platform are not official study material. They are written from my personal understanding and may contain errors or simplifications. Always cross-reference with your official textbooks and syllabus. I am not responsible for any inaccuracies.
